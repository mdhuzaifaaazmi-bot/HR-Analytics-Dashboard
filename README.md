# HR-Analytics-Dashboard
This project is an HR Analytics Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends within an organization. The main objective of the project is to help the HR department understand the major factors affecting employee turnover and support better business decisions through data-driven insights. The dashboard presents important KPIs such as total employee count, attrition count, attrition rate, average salary, average employee age, and average years spent at the company.

The dashboard provides a detailed analysis of employee attrition based on various factors including education field, age group, department, salary slab, and job role. The analysis shows that employees in the Research & Development department had the highest attrition count, while employees between the age group of 26–35 showed the maximum turnover. The project also highlights that employees with lower salary slabs experienced higher attrition rates, helping organizations identify possible retention challenges.

The project uses interactive visualizations and charts to make complex HR data easy to understand and interpret. Through this dashboard, HR teams can identify patterns, monitor employee behavior, and develop strategies to improve employee satisfaction and reduce attrition. The dashboard also enables management to compare attrition trends across departments and roles, helping them take proactive actions for workforce management.

This project demonstrates practical skills in data analysis, business intelligence, and data visualization using Power BI. It also showcases knowledge of data cleaning, data transformation, KPI generation, and DAX functions. Overall, the HR Analytics Dashboard is a valuable business intelligence solution that transforms raw employee data into meaningful insights for organizational growth and employee retention.
